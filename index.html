01 — About Us

A quiet house of
Vietnamese coffee.

ETB Global is the consumer face of E-TBGlobal Trading and Services Joint Stock Company, a Vietnamese enterprise devoted to the craft of premium roasted coffee.

We believe every coffee bean carries more than flavor — it carries the essence of Vietnamese culture, the passion of its growers, and the patience of a centuries-old ritual. Our role is to honor that inheritance and bring it, unfiltered, to the cups of consumers around the world.

From origin to roast, each batch is curated with the quiet precision of a house that understands coffee as heritage — refined, authentic, and made to travel well.
